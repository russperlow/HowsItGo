How's It Go? Is a website designed to get you the lyrics to your favorite songs from Spotify.

All you need to do is sign into your Spotify Premium account (playing music will not work without Spotify Premium), pick a playlist and a song and we will do our best to find the lyrics for you!

How's It Go? uses the Spotify API and Orion Apiseeds Lyrics in order to bring you content.
